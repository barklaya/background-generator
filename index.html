Georgy Barklaya *1990 in Moscow, Russia Lives and works in Prague, Czech Repulic

He has been working in the film industry since 2015 namely in the world of visual effects surrounded by modern technologies. He has had the opportunity to work on various well-known international feature films and TV shows in his capacity as data manager and VFX editor at UPP Post Production House. He also has an experience as a 3D scanning technician, where his main responsibility was converting real objects into a digital form. Apart of laser and photogrammetry capture of objects as film sets or props, he was also responsible for the 360 degree photo booth which takes pictures of actors with following creating their digital doubles for its subsequent use in VFX shots. Projects he has been involved with using these technologies are Anthem: Conviction, Carnival Row, Gemini Man, Terminator: Dark Fate, etc.. I currently collaborate with Creativepictures.cz as a camera and steadicam operator, something that I’m particularly excited about as it feeds my passion for working behind the camera.

For me it’s all about capturing the moment by expressing feelings and emotions through images – all with the aim of conveying potent non-verbal messages. Through my work I’ve had the privilege of getting to know the world and people around me, which encourages me to seek out new experiences and ideas.

I’m always looking to develop myself both creatively and technically. But above all to take inspiring, aesthetic images that awaken real feelings within people, that leave a lasting impression.
